Questions

Everything you need to know

What do I need to get started? +
Just sign up with your email, add your WhatsApp number, and create your first unit. NestMind AI connects through the Evolution API to handle WhatsApp messages automatically. No special hardware or software needed.
How does the AI handle tenant messages? +
NestMind AI uses an agentic framework powered by Google Gemini. When a tenant messages, the AI triages the request, classifies severity, checks legal compliance for your province, and drafts a reply — all in seconds. You can review drafts before they're sent, or enable autopilot for routine issues.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Multi-tenant architecture ensures complete isolation between landlords. Utility credentials are encrypted and never exposed to the AI — only used internally for automated scraping.
Can I control what the AI sends to tenants? +
Absolutely. You control the auto-reply toggle, the delay before replies are sent, and the cooldown between messages. For high-severity issues, the AI always alerts you first and waits for approval. You can also set it to full autopilot for routine requests.
What provinces/states are supported? +
NestMind AI includes legal compliance profiles for 12 jurisdictions: Ontario, British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba, Quebec, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, New York, California, Texas, and the United Kingdom. More are being added regularly.
What if the AI makes a mistake? +
Every AI draft goes through legal compliance checks before being sent. For critical issues, the landlord is always notified first. You can review, edit, or reject any draft from the dashboard. The AI also logs every step it takes so you can audit its reasoning.
